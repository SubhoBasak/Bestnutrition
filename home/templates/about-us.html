Company Profile


Dr. Abhay Kumar Pati, also known as Dr. Kumar Pati, is the founder of Best Nutrition Products, Inc., USA. The company was started by Dr. Pati in 1994. Prior to establishing Best Nutrition Products, Inc. Dr. Pati was engaged in publishing a health magazine - known as “Health World”, which had a large circulation. As the internet became more popular, his magazine readership declined. Subsequently, he formulated a few health supplements. Currently, Best Nutrition Products has over 200 health products


Dr. Pati is an integrated physician, who studied both modern medicine and the traditional medicine of India, Ayurvedic medicine. A graduate of J. B. Roy State College of Ayurvedic Medicine & Hospital, (known also as Asthanga Ayurvedic College) in Kolkata India, was one of India's oldest educational institution in Ayurvedic medicine. This college was founded in 1916 by Doctor and Pandit Jamini Bhushan Ray, Mahatma Gandhi and Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel.


Dr. Abhay Kumar Pati (BAMS, MASF ) is the key and sole founder of Biotechayur R&D laboratory and Biotechayur manufacturing unit .


Dr. Abhay Kumar Pati is an author of half a dozen books on nutrition and vitamins. Titles include: Ayurvedic Medicinal Plants of India, Vitamins & Herbal Digest, A Guide to Alternative Health Therapies, Medicinal Plants of India and Fruits of the World. These publications are available on Amazon.com and 72 other websites.
